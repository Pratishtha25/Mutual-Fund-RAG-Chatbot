Scheme Name: Groww Large Cap Fund
Document: Scheme Information & Factsheet Summary (2026)
Asset Management Company: Groww Asset Management Company (Groww AMC)

1. Expense Ratio:
The Total Expense Ratio (TER) charged by the scheme is 1.38% for the Direct Plan and 2.74% for the Regular Plan. This includes the investment management fee and operating administrative expenses.

2. Exit Load:
If units are redeemed or switched out within 7 days from the date of allotment, an exit load of 1.00% is applicable. No exit load is charged for redemptions made after 7 days from the date of allotment.

3. Minimum Investment:
The minimum application amount required for a Lump Sum investment is ₹1,000. The minimum SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) amount is ₹500 per month.

4. Lock-in Period:
Being an open-ended equity scheme, Groww Large Cap Fund does not have any lock-in period. Investors are free to redeem their units on any business day, subject to applicable exit loads.

5. Riskometer & Risk Classification:
The riskometer classification for Groww Large Cap Fund is categorized as Very High Risk, indicating that investors understand their principal will be at very high risk.

6. Benchmark Index:
The scheme's performance and asset allocation are benchmarked against the NIFTY 100 TRI (Total Returns Index).

7. Assets Under Management (AUM) & Fund Size:
The Assets Under Management (AUM) of the Groww Large Cap Fund is ₹1,250 Crores as of May 31, 2026. The fund is managed under Groww Asset Management Company.

8. Fund Manager Details:
The fund is managed by Mr. Anupam Tiwari. Mr. Tiwari holds a PGDM in Finance and has extensive experience in equity research and fund management.

9. Fund Category:
Groww Large Cap Fund belongs to the Equity: Large Cap category, investing predominantly in equity and equity-related instruments of large-cap companies.

10. Investment Objectives:
The investment objective of the scheme is to generate long-term capital appreciation by investing in a diversified portfolio of large-cap companies.

11. Document Access & Statement Downloads:
To download the Scheme Information Document (SID), Key Information Memorandum (KIM), or your Consolidated Account Statement (CAS), visit the official Groww Mutual Fund website or go to the Groww app under the Mutual Fund Profile & Statements section.
