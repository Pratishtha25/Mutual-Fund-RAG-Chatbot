Scheme Name: Axis Long Term Equity Fund
Document: ELSS Scheme Factsheet Summary (2026)
Asset Management Company: Axis Mutual Fund AMC

1. Expense Ratio:
The Total Expense Ratio (TER) is 0.85% for the Direct Plan and 1.60% for the Regular Plan.

2. Exit Load:
There is Nil exit load. However, early redemption is not permitted due to the regulatory lock-in period.

3. Minimum Investment:
The minimum application amount is ₹500 and in multiples of ₹500 thereafter for both Lump Sum and SIP investments.

4. Lock-in Period:
Axis Long Term Equity Fund is an Equity Linked Savings Scheme (ELSS). It carries a mandatory lock-in period of 3 years (36 months) from the date of unit allotment. During this lock-in period, units cannot be redeemed, transferred, or pledged.

5. Riskometer & Risk Classification:
Categorized as Very High Risk as per SEBI riskometer guidelines.

6. Benchmark Index:
The scheme tracks the Nifty 500 TRI as its official benchmark index.

7. Assets Under Management (AUM) & Fund Size:
The Assets Under Management (AUM) of the Axis Long Term Equity Fund is ₹24,600 Crores as of May 31, 2026.

8. Fund Manager Details:
The fund is managed by Mr. Shreyash Devalkar, who has been managing the portfolio since 2016. He holds a Bachelor of Chemical Engineering and a PGDM, with more than 18 years of capital market experience.

9. Document Access & Statement Downloads:
To download your ELSS 80C Tax Investment Proof, Account Statement, or SID, visit the Axis Mutual Fund customer portal or navigate to Groww App > Profile > Tax Statements.
