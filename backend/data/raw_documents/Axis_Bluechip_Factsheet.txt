Scheme Name: Axis Bluechip Fund
Document: Scheme Information & Factsheet Summary (2026)
Asset Management Company: Axis Mutual Fund AMC

1. Expense Ratio:
The Total Expense Ratio (TER) charged by the scheme is 0.90% for the Direct Plan and 1.65% for the Regular Plan. This includes the investment management fee and operating administrative expenses.

2. Exit Load:
If units are redeemed or switched out within 1 year (365 days) from the date of allotment, an exit load of 1.00% is applicable. No exit load is charged for redemptions made after 1 year (365 days) from the date of allotment.

3. Minimum Investment:
The minimum application amount required for a Lump Sum investment is ₹5,000 and in multiples of ₹1 thereafter. The minimum SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) amount is ₹500 per month and in multiples of ₹1 thereafter.

4. Lock-in Period:
Being an open-ended equity scheme, Axis Bluechip Fund does not have any lock-in period. Investors are free to redeem their units on any business day, subject to applicable exit loads.

5. Riskometer & Risk Classification:
The riskometer classification for Axis Bluechip Fund is categorized as Very High Risk, indicating that investors understand their principal will be at very high risk.

6. Benchmark Index:
The scheme's performance and asset allocation are benchmarked against the Nifty 50 TRI (Total Returns Index).

7. Assets Under Management (AUM) & Fund Size:
The Assets Under Management (AUM) of the Axis Bluechip Fund is ₹35,820 Crores as of May 31, 2026. The fund is managed under Axis Asset Management Company Limited.

8. Fund Manager Details:
The fund is managed by Mr. Jinesh Gopani, who has been leading the scheme since November 2016. Mr. Gopani holds a Master of Management Studies (MMS) in Finance and has over 20 years of extensive experience in equity research, portfolio management, and financial analysis.

9. Document Access & Statement Downloads:
To download the Scheme Information Document (SID), Key Information Memorandum (KIM), or your Consolidated Account Statement (CAS), visit the official Axis Mutual Fund website or go to the Groww app under the Mutual Fund Profile & Statements section.
