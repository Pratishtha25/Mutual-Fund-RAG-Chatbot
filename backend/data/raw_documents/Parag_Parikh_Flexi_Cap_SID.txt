Scheme Name: Parag Parikh Flexi Cap Fund
Document: Scheme Information Document Summary (2026)
Asset Management Company: PPFAS Mutual Fund AMC

1. Expense Ratio:
The scheme's Total Expense Ratio (TER) is 0.70% for the Direct Plan and 1.45% for the Regular Plan, which covers administrative, distributor commission, and fund management expenses.

2. Exit Load:
The exit load structure is designed to encourage long-term investing:
- Redemption within 365 days (1 year) from allotment: 2.00%
- Redemption between 366 days and 730 days (2 years) from allotment: 1.00%
- Redemption after 730 days (2 years): Nil exit load.

3. Minimum Investment:
The minimum initial application amount for Lump Sum is ₹1,000 and in multiples of ₹1 thereafter. The minimum monthly SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) amount is ₹1,000.

4. Lock-in Period:
Parag Parikh Flexi Cap Fund has no lock-in period. It is an open-ended dynamic equity scheme investing across large cap, mid cap, and small cap stocks.

5. Riskometer & Risk Classification:
The riskometer classification for this scheme is Very High Risk, indicating high volatility in the short term.

6. Benchmark Index:
The benchmark index for Parag Parikh Flexi Cap Fund is the Nifty 500 TRI (Total Returns Index), which represents a broad-based market coverage.

7. Assets Under Management (AUM) & Fund Size:
The Assets Under Management (AUM) is ₹52,400 Crores as of May 31, 2026, managed under PPFAS Asset Management Private Limited.

8. Fund Manager Details:
The equity portion is managed by Mr. Rajeev Thakkar, who has been managing the scheme since its inception in May 2013. He is a Chartered Accountant (CA) and holds a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) charter, with over 25 years of research and fund management experience.

9. Document Access & Statement Downloads:
To download scheme pamphlets, KIMs, SIDs, or tax statements, log in to the PPFAS AMC investor portal or access the Groww App dashboard under mutual fund downloads.
